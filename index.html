AKAN NAMES

This is an application where you input your date of birth and the year to give you an akan name according to the day you were born

BIRTHDAY INFORMATION

You have to enter your birth details in this format (mm/dd/yy).
  • dd=The day
  • mm=The month
  • yyyy=The year

GENDER

choose your gender either Male or Female field provided since your akan name will be outputed only when you have provided the gender and your valid dates so you need to understand that the gender section is important.
